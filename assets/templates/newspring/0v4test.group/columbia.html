What can I expect?

Sunday services at NewSpring are exciting, casual and relaxed. Come as you are, and you can expect to feel welcomed, experience good music, and an engaging message from our Senior Pastor Perry Noble or other guest speakers.

Where can I park?

The Columbia Campus has two parking lots, on either side of the campus. Family parking is close to our Children's Ministry check-in, to get you an your family settled in quickly before the service.
