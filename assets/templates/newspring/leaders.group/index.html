Leadership Team

Perry Noble

Senior Pastor

Perry Noble is the founding and senior pastor of NewSpring Church in South Carolina. The church averages 32,000 people during weekend services at multiple campuses throughout the state.

Perry is a gifted communicator and teacher, convicted about speaking the truth as plainly as possible. God has given him a vision and a passion for helping people meet Jesus, and each week he shares God’s Word and its practical application in our daily lives.

Perry, his wife Lucretia, and their daughter Charisse live in Anderson, South Carolina. You can read all of Perry’s unfiltered thoughts about life and leadership at PerryNoble.com. Don’t worry, he holds nothing back.

Brad
Cooper

Family Ministries Pastor
"The wave starts in the student section."

Brad oversees KidSpring and Fuse, two of NewSpring’s largest ministries. Brad has a passion for the next generation and seeing students’ and children’s lives changed by Jesus Christ. Brad is the primary communicator for Fuse and has a unique ability of connecting with the students of NewSpring Church. Brad has an amazing wife named Cory. They enjoy Clemson football and spending time with family and friends.

Shane
Duffey

Creative Arts Pastor
"I love NewSpring Church because it’s a church where change happens!"

Shane, his wife Cherie, and their four sons started attending NewSpring in 2003. He’s been on staff since 2005. In addition to serving on our Directional Leadership Team, Shane works with the teams that handle Events, Public Relations, and produce our Sunday services each week to create a church experience that is engaging to those who are unfamiliar with church and empowering to those who have attended church for a long time. The goal is for everyone to encounter Jesus.

Paul
Marshall

Financial Pastor
"There is nothing better than being used in a positive way in a move of God."

Paul met Jesus in his small town church when he was 12 years old. After working as a small businessman for 22 years, Paul accepted a challenge from Perry to come on staff in 2004. Paul lives with Beth, his wife of 32 years, and they are enjoying the empty nest. They have three children, two children-in-laws, and two grandchildren.

Campus Pastors

Riley Cummings

Anderson Campus

Riley received Christ at an early age, but it was at NewSpring that he learned to love and pursue the Lord. He began attending the Anderson Campus as a student at Clemson and the Lord used the people there to change his life. Riley has always loved the Anderson and Clemson community, and the opportunity to serve as a Campus Pastor is his dream job.

Cam O'Sullivan

Boiling Springs Campus

Cam was born and raised in Spartanburg. He accepted Christ and was baptized at an early age. Because of the influence of his teammates and coaches on the Clemson football team, Cam started attending NewSpring on a regular basis. He decided to become an Owner in the spring of 2009. Greeting at the Anderson Campus led to greeting at the Greenville campus after graduation. When the Spartanburg Campus launched, Cam was a greeter, KidSpring advocate, and Fuse volunteer. He then came on staff in July 2013 as Spartanburg's associate campus pastor and became the Boiling Springs campus pastor in October of 2013. Cam lives in Boiling Springs with his wife, Emily, and their daughter.

Cliff Craig

Clemson Campus

Cliff was saved by Jesus at an early age, but didn't fully understand what it meant to follow Jesus until being challenged by some friends when he started attending Clemson University. The same friends invited him to NewSpring, and he began attending the Anderson Campus in the early 2000's. It was around that same time that he felt the Lord calling him into ministry. Cliff is married to Lauren, and they have three daughters. This is Cliff's dream job: serving Jesus in the best church in the world, in the most incredible location in the world.
