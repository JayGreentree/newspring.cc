Responsibilities of a Host


  • Invite.

    Friends, family, and neighbors can all join your group. The best way to fill your group is by asking the people God has intentionally placed in your life.

  • Facilitate.

    There are discussion questions for the group to go over following up on the video teaching. It is up to the HOST to help people start discussing.

  • Open.

    Your home will be the meeting place for your group over 6 weeks.

  • Watch.

    There will be video teaching on a DVD that you and your group will need to watch each week. Make sure you have a DVD player ready to go!

  • Respond.

    Make sure that you contact anyone that signs up for your group within 48 hours of receiving the email. Also, we want to stay in the loop with who is in your group, and how everything is going.

  • Don'ts

  • Greet guests at the door as they arrive and introduce yourself!

  • Make sure your living room and bathrooms are clean to create a welcoming environment.

  • Start and end on time to respect everyone’s time.

  • Look through the content beforehand and be prepared to lead the discussions.

  • Maybe have some snacks or coffee for everyone!

  • Be prepared to have a lot of fun, to make new friendships, and see people take next steps!

  • Pray for your group.

  • Ask friends and neighbors to join your group.

  • Make sure everyone knows the group details before the first group meets (time, location, etc).

  • Think about any questions you can ask that are "open ended" to foster group discussion.

  • Rush around the house to get everything in order while people are arriving!

  • Give off the impression that there will be snacks and then not have any! Nobody focuses well on an empty stomach!

  • Forget to look over the content and wing it after watching the DVD!

  • Suddenly forget how to work the DVD player! Practice before everyone arrives to make sure everything is working!

  • Have your pets running around the house. We know you love your pet, but for the sake of those in your group who may not please have a plan for them.

  • Forget to think about how you are going to break the ice and then awkwardly stumble through the first few minutes of your group time.

  • Assume that everyone is going to talk. (Encourage people by going first in conversation and asking open ended questions)

  • Talk the whole time. It is important for you as the HOST to allow people to speak. Quietness for you as a HOST might feel uncomfortable, but allow that and then someone will talk.

  • Don't speak your "opinion" too soon, because others might shut down and not talk.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Host?

A Host is any NewSpring Owner who is willing to open their home up once a week for six weeks and invite others to go through the Step Up group curriculum together. The Host will be responsible for contacting group members to set up the weekly meetings as well as facilitating the discussions that go along with the group guides.

How do I become a Host?

Any NewSpring owner can sign up by clicking the link above and attending one of the Step Up Host trainings over this summer at your campus!

Who will I host in my group?

We encourage you to begin personally inviting friends, family, and neighbors to be in your group this fall. We want anyone who attends or is interested in NewSpring to join us in these groups! We will open group sign ups online and start promoting in service on July 21st. You can let us know if you want your group open for sign ups and anyone interested can join your group.

What if I am already in a group?

The group that you are currently in at NewSpring will be going through this content at the same time as the rest of the church. If you feel led to be a Host of a new group for those six weeks then we would love for you to sign up and let us know!

What happens if no one signs up for my group?

If there aren’t enough people to fill your group, we will combine your group with another group that is also looking for additional people. We will contact you by the first week of August if this happens.

When do these groups begin?

All Step Up groups will begin the week after Sunday, August 11th.

What if people have questions I cant answer?

Don't worry; you don't need to know all of the answers! We will consistently post helpful information and resources. You can also email us questions anytime.
