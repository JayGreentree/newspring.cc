Ladies and gentleman, start your engines! Coming to your campus in July is the family event of the summer at NewSpring Church—the Race Day 500 Festival. Bring everyone you know for food, fun and fierce competition of lawn mower races!

Lawn Mower Race Registration

  • You must be at least 18 years old to participate.
  • You must have a valid driver's license to participate.
  • You are responsible for bringing your personal lawn mower.
  • You must sign a waiver and bring it with you to your event.
  • A prize will be awarded to the winner from your campus.
