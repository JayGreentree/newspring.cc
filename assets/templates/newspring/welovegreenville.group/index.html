About NewSpring Church

NewSpring Church exists to reach people far from God and to teach them how to follow Jesus step by step. We currently have 8 locations across the state of South Carolina. We believe that no matter how long we have been following Jesus, every one of us has a next step to take in our walk with Christ.

About the Greenville Campus

On January 16, 2000 NewSpring began meeting on the campus of Anderson College in Anderson, South Carolina. As the church grew we saw more and more people driving to Anderson from Greenville. In an effort to reach the 500,000 people of Greenville County we decided to try something we had never attempted before.

We leased a former grocery store at the intersection of Roper Mountain Road and I-385, built a 1,200 seat auditorium and installed video technology allowing us to broadcast our messages live from the Anderson Campus to the Greenville Campus. On July 13, 2008, NewSpring launched its first multisite campus with 1,710 in attendance on opening weekend. With many asking, “Will it work?” the results began to speak for themselves. Six months in, a third service was added to accommodate the campus’ growth.

In the spring of 2009, due to circumstances beyond our control, we learned that we had to vacate our building. A few short weeks after receiving the news, NewSpring Greenville did something few churches do: on June 14, 2009 it relocated from a permanent facility to a portable location. Not fully understanding why God had allowed this to happen, we trusted that He had better things in store for the Greenville campus.

Since moving to the TD Center, located off of Pleasantburg Drive, we have experienced amazing growth with an average weekly attendance of 4,943 spread across four weekend services. NewSpring Greenville has grown into one of the largest portable church campuses in America.

What started as a huge vision and a huge risk has proven to be more successful than anyone could have dreamed or imagined. Since its launch, NewSpring Greenville has seen 3,953 people meet Jesus, 2,010 baptized, and countless lives changed.

Greenville Campus has led the way in NewSpring Church’s multisite vision to reach 100,000 people across the state of South Carolina and the best is yet to come!
