"Can God Be Trusted?"

NewSpring Greenville 5 Year Anniversary Celebration

What would you attempt for God if you knew you couldn't fail? For NewSpring, an answer to that question came five years ago as we attempted something few thought would work: we went multi-site and launched a campus in Greenville. Since then, we have seen Jesus do unbelievable things to build His Church in Greenville—restore relationships, heal

hurts, and save thousands. We think that's worth celebrating! Join us on Sunday, November 10th at the Bon Secours Wellness Arena for a time of celebration and a special in person message from our Pastor, Perry Noble, as we remember all that God has done over the past five years and anticipate what's ahead because the best is yet to come!

Event Details

Join us for a time of celebration and hearing Pastor Perry Noble live in person at the Bon Secours Wellness Arena on Sunday, November 10th at 11:00am!

Location & Times

Bon Secours Wellness Arena, formerly the Bi-Lo Center in Greenville, SC Map
