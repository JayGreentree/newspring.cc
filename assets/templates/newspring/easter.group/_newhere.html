About NewSpring

NewSpring Church exists to reach people far from God and teach them how to follow Jesus step by step. We have nine campuses across the state of South Carolina. We'd love for you to be our guest.
Find a location near you or watch live.

What to Expect

Sunday services at NewSpring are exciting, casual and relaxed. Come as you are, and you can expect to feel welcomed, experience good music, and an engaging message from our Senior Pastor Perry Noble or other guest speakers.

For Kids

For babies–5th grade, KidSpring is a children’s ministry uniquely designed with your child in mind! Kids experience safe, age-appropriate environments where the Bible is taught in a creative and relevant way. Learn more about KidSpring.

For Students

For students in grades 6–12, we’re dedicated to providing a place for them to connect with God, with other students, and with older committed leaders who serve faithfully to see students grow closer to Christ.
