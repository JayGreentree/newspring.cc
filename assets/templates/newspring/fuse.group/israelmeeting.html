What's This Class About

The Fuse to Israel Parent Meeting is a 27 minute video of Pastor Perry and Jason Moorhead answering the questions you have been asking. In this time you'll hear answers to questions regarding safety, food, transportation, payment, and an overview of what this trip will look like for the students going. You'll also hear the vision behind this incredible opportunity for our high schoolers in Fuse!

If you're interested in going to Israel or you're a parent wanting more information, this video is for you!
