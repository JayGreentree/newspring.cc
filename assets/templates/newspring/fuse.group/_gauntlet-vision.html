What is The Gauntlet?

The Gauntlet is a five-day trip for middle and high school students to Daytona Beach, FL. Throughout the week, students will learn about and experience God through sessions led by Brad Cooper, our Fuse student pastor, Perry Noble, our senior pastor, and through worship led by our NewSpring Fuse band. Students will also get to be a part of small groups led by our trained volunteers where they can share and talk through everything the Lord is teaching them.

The Gauntlet is Sold Out!

You can still add your name to the Waitlist and we’ll contact you directly if extra tickets become available at your campus. Adding your name to the Waitlist does not guarantee you a ticket.

Why does NewSpring have The Gauntlet?

NewSpring believes in pouring into the next generation and teaching them to love and follow Jesus with their whole hearts. You can hear Pastor Perry's explanation from a message on March 3, 2013, for the Gauntlet here.

  • 1

    Create a focused, distraction-free environment where students can hear the truth of God's Word.

  • 2

    Challenge students in their own personal walks with Jesus.

  • 3

    Have a blast hanging out with other students at the beach!
