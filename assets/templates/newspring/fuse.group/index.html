What is Fuse?

Fuse is the weekly gathering of the NewSpring Student Ministry. We’re dedicated to providing a place for students (6th -12th grade) to connect with God, with other students, and with older committed leaders that are dedicated to seeing students grow closer to Christ.

Who Can Attend Fuse?

Any students in grades 6–12 may attend Fuse.

Join a Fuse Group

At NewSpring Church, we believe that you can't do life alone. At Fuse, we believe the same. We want to pair you with a group of guys or girls your same age/grade so that you can have people to talk with, pray with and simply "do life" with.

If you are a part of Fuse and are interested in joining a small group, it is as easy as filling out this form and someone will contact you very soon to get you all set up.
