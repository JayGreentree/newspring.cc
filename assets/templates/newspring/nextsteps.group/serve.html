Serving Information

At NewSpring, we believe that saved people serve people. God has given each person a gift to equip us to serve so that the body of Christ may be built up. There are many opportunities to serve at NewSpring and we want you to find the one that best suits your passion, personality and availability.

The first step to serving at NewSpring, is to attend Ownership Class. If you have already joined NewSpring and are ready to serve, click one of the ministries to the right to begin serving or to simply learn more. If you aren't sure which ministry is best for you, click "I'm not sure" and someone will be in touch with you to help you take your next step.
