Members have rights; Owners have responsibilities

We believe every Christ-follower should belong to a local church and take ownership of that church. Ownership at NewSpring includes serving, giving and participating in biblical community.

We offer monthly Ownership classes where you'll learn the vision and values of NewSpring. At Ownership Class we'll teach you the five core values our church lives by: Found People Find People, Saved People Serve People, Growing People Change, You Can't Do Life Alone and You Can't Outgive God. At the end of the class you'll have an opportunity to become an owner of NewSpring Church.

Ownership Class is designed for 6th grade (12 years-old) and up. Sign up at Guest Services on Sunday or by using one of the links below. This class is a requirement for ownership.

At NewSpring Church, we're working to reach 100,000 people for Jesus Christ. Will you join us?
