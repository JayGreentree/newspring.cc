Care & Prayer Information

We believe that everyone can walk in freedom. We would love to be able to talk and pray with you about what is going on in your life and help you discover your next step.

We have a Care Team at each campus that would love to talk with you, provide biblical guidance, and pray for or with you. Our Care Team is available after every service on Sunday in our Care and Prayer Room. In addition, you can also request to speak with someone or ask for prayer here.
