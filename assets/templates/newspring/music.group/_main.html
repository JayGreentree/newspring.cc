What can you expect to hear at NewSpring?

We’ll use multiple musical styles and songs, performed with world class excellence. We’ll play songs you’d hear on the radio. We’ll sing hymns that have been sung for centuries, worship songs we’ve written, and songs from other churches around the world; all with the goal of helping people just like you encounter Jesus.
