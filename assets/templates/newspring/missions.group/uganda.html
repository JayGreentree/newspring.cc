About the Trip

At NewSpring Church we are partnering with local churches across the world to reach people for Jesus. NewSpring Church is partnering with Freedom Church and planning several trips over the next few years. You can find out more about Freedom Church here.

We will be going to Kampala, Uganda. As the capital of Uganda, Kampala is an urban area with over 1.7 million people living in the city. It’s located just north of the equator, so don’t forget your sunscreen! We will work with the Kampala campus of Freedom Church to help change their community and reach people for Jesus. If you’re interested in going with us to Uganda on one of the upcoming trips, you can sign up below.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the age requirement to participate on a NewSpring Missions Trip?

For these trips we ask for the participants to be at least 16 years old. Anyone under the age of 18 must have parental consent to participate on a trip.

Do I have to be an Owner of NewSpring Church to apply for the trip?

No, you do not have to be a NewSpring Owner to apply. Learn more about Ownership at NewSpring.

Do I have to attend NewSpring Church to apply for a trip?

Those that attend our campuses will be given a higher priority than those who don’t. You must be willing to attend and be an active part of the team meetings (approximately three meetings).

What is the cost of a trip?

The cost of the trip is $3,500. It includes airfare, hotel, meals, and transportation while in Uganda. You will be responsible for your meals and snacks in the airports and for any souvenirs you would like to purchase. Additional cost may include an up-to-date passport, any shots you choose to get, and $50 for a visa upon arrival in Uganda.

Is a passport required?

Yes. Traveling outside the United States requires an up-to-date passport.

I did not receive a confirmation email. Did my application go through?

No. Please fill out the application again. You will receive a confirmation email once your application is submitted.

What happens to my application if I’m not selected for a specific trip?

If you are not selected your application goes onto a waiting list. If another trip leader is still looking for people for their team, then they may contact you to see if you are interested.

Have More Questions?

If we did not answer your questions about the trip, please email us for more details.
