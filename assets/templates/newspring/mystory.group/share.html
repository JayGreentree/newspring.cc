Share Your Story With Us

Jesus changes lives, and we want to celebrate that as a church by sharing more video stories on our website and in our services. We're looking for all sorts of stories of how God has been moving in the lives of our members and attenders:

  • In rescue, such as salvation in Jesus or freedom from a particular sin or healing from a broken situation.
  • In loving care, such as an answered prayer, a divine appointment or an instance of God's special favor.
  • In mission, such as volunteering, serving the community or bringing others closer to Jesus.

We can't promise every story will be told on video, but we will share as many as possible with NewSpring staff and volunteers to encourage them in their ministry!
