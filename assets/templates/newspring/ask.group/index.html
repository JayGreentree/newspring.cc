About NewSpring Church

NewSpring Church exists to reach people far from God and teach them to follow Jesus step by step.

Find a Location

We have 8 campuses across the state of South Carolina.

What to Expect

Sunday services at NewSpring are exciting, casual and relaxed. Come as you are, and you can expect to feel welcomed, experience good music, and an engaging message from our Senior Pastor Perry Noble or other guest speakers.

For Families

KidSpring is a children’s ministry for babies through 5th grade that is uniquely designed with your child in mind! Kids experience safe, age-appropriate environments where the Bible is taught in a creative and relevant way. Learn more about KidSpring.

For Students

Fuse is a student ministry for students in grades 6–12 that is dedicated to providing a place for them to connect with God, with other students, and with older committed leaders who serve faithfully to see students grow closer to Christ.
Learn more about Fuse.
