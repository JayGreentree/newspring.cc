Service Time Changes

NewSpring Church exists to reach people far from God and teach them to follow Jesus step by step. We know the game is important so we're moving the service times to better fit your schedule. This will allow us to reach more people that week and give you more opportunities to invite your friends and family. Check out the different times below for details.

If your campus has a night service on Sunday, those services will move to Saturday night.

Saturday Service Times

5:00PM & 6:30PM

Sunday Service Times

8:30AM, 10:00AM & 11:30AM

BSP, CHS, FLO and MYR will not have Saturday night services
