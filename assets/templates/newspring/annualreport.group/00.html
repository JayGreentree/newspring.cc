2012 Annual Report

Wow!!! 2012 was truly an amazing year for our church... more than we could ask or imagine! More than 6400 people asked Jesus into their lives. More than 3200 people took their next step to go public with baptism. We launched three more campuses this year. And we saw our average weekly attendance increase 39% from 2011.

Our Annual Report is always full of numbers. But these numbers are so important to our church because every number has a name, every name has a story and every story matters to God.

Jesus is changing lives in and through our church and the exciting thing is that He's just getting started! I'm so thankful to serve this church as the pastor and I really do believe the best is yet to come!

Perry Noble, Senior Pastor
