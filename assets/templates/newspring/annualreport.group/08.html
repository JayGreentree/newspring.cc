KidSpring Vision & Mission

KidSpring is a children’s ministry uniquely designed with your child in mind! Kids—birth through fifth grade—experience safe, age-appropriate environments where the Bible is taught in a creative and relevant way.

KidSpring Resources for Churches

In 2012 we began creating our own children’s curriculum to teach kids about Jesus on their level. We also began giving that curriculum away—for free—to other churches. These resources include lesson plans, training documents, songs and videos. 16,783 people have registered to download resources!
