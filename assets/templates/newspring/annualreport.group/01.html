Ephesians 3:20 tells us Jesus is able to do immeasurably more than we can ask or imagine and we've seen that in our church in 2013. 11,983 people asked Jesus to come into their lives at NewSpring and 6551 people took their next step of baptism across our state this year! This is NOT normal!!!

These numbers, and the ones following in this year’s Annual Report, are important because they represent people and the life change that’s happening in our church. Every number has a name, every name has a story, and every story matters to God. Because of this we’re always going to celebrate numbers at NewSpring!

Jesus is changing our state and we’re on our way to reaching 100,000 people, but the exciting thing is it’s just the beginning. I truly do believe the BEST IS YET TO COME and I’m so honored and thankful to be your pastor!!! I love my church!!!

Perry Noble, Senior Pastor
