What's this Class About?

Unleash Your Finances is a 45-minute teaching session with Pastor Perry Noble that covers five specific next steps you can take to completely revolutionize your personal finances.

If you’re confused, stressed or overwhelmed about money, this class will help. God wants to do more than you can ask for or imagine with it. Learn how to have the abundant life Jesus promised when you turn your finances from worry into worship.

  • 3

  • 4
    How to be in control of your money instead of letting your money control you.
  • 5
    How to stop fighting about money in your marriage.

Why is Money So Important?

Money is one of the most confusing and stressful parts of our daily lives. But money is also one of the topics that God makes very clear in the Bible.

The Bible contains less than 500 verses on faith and about 500 verses on prayer, but more than 2,000 verses about money and possessions. In fact, Jesus talked more about money more than He talked about Heaven and Hell—combined. Why? Because He knew that money would always be the single biggest competitor for our heart, attention and worship.
