Spartanburg Campus Building Opening
March 23rd, 2014

NewSpring Church exists to reach people far from God and teach them to follow Jesus step by step. We are on a mission to reach 100,000 people for Jesus in South Carolina. That's why we're moving our Spartanburg campus into a permanent location on March 23rd, 2014. This building provides much needed room to grow with a 1,400 seat auditorium and 25,500 sq.ft. dedicated to teaching kids about Jesus on their level in KidSpring.
