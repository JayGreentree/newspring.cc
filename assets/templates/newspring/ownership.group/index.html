Why Ownership?

Members have rights, Owners have responsibilities. We offer monthly Ownership classes where you'll learn the vision and values of NewSpring. We’ll explain the five core values our church lives by: Found People Find People, Saved People Serve People, Growing People Change, You Can’t Do Life Alone, and You Can’t Outgive God.

Ownership Class is designed for 6th grade (12 years-old) and up. Sign up at your campus on Sunday or by using one of the links below. This class is a requirement for Ownership. At the end of the class you’ll have an opportunity to become an owner of NewSpring Church.

At NewSpring Church, we’re working to reach 100,000 people in South Carolina with the gospel of Jesus. We’d love for you to join us!
