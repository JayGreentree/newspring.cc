Session 1

Change Your Financial Future!

What do you need to change this year to bring your finances more in line with God’s work in your life? Is your financial preparation showing that you expect God to do big things in your life or are your stuck in a debt trap? In this session, Joseph Sangl teaches four core principles to empower you to find freedom in your finances and begin to win with money, God’s way.

Session 2

How to be Rich - 9 Rungs to a Fully Funded Life

Living a fully-funded life means you have the ability to do exactly what you’ve been put on earth to do, regardless of the cost. No matter where you currently are with your finances, you can live a fully-funded life! Joseph Sangl explains in this session the nine rungs of the ladder you can climb and ways that you can remain stable with your finances once you've reached the top rung.

Links for this session: Saving Tools

Session 3

How To Get Paid When You're Not Working

There are only two possible outcomes for your finances: emptiness or abundance. The key to living abundantly with your finances is ensuring that you have enough oxen in your life to lead the way to financial freedom through hard work and inspired effort. Do you have oxen in your life? Are you taking care of the oxen you have?

Watch Video

Session 4

Funding Your Plans, Hopes, and Dreams

Do you have a big dream that you’re scared to talk about because you’re sure someone will say you’re dumb for dreaming it? The great news is that God is in the miracle business and He wants you to have big dreams that will bring Him glory! But we have to start preparing financially, trusting that God wants to work miracles in our lives and make our big dreams happen. Do you know how much the big dream God has given you will cost? Are you financially ready it?

Session 5

Budgeting

Are you scared of budgeting? Does a budget seem pointless to you? Think again! A budget is the engine of financial planning that will propel you toward your financial plans, hopes and dreams. In this session, Joseph Sangl tells you the importance of budgeting and gives tips on how to successfully plan your money so that you can anticipate God’s blessings in your finances.

Links for this session: Budgeting Tools

Session 6

Debt Elimination

No matter how big it is, you can be freed from your debt! Having debt freedom will transform how you view money and how you live your life. In this session, Joseph Sangl explains how to use a Debt Freedom Calculator and talks about the importance of setting a date to be free from your debt.

Session 7

Retirement Calculation

It’s a day that comes for all of us at one point in our lives. After years of work, we get tired and we all want to do one thing: retire. And when retirement comes, you leave behind one of two things: an inheritance for your children’s children or a legacy of debt. Are you on track to live and pass on a financially blessed life?

Session 8

Compound Interest and Investing

Are your current investments matching the work of God? Are you living in the belief that Jesus is enough and God owns your finances? In this session, Joseph Sangl wraps up by talking about compound interest and how having it helps push us toward financial success.

Joseph Sangl is a leading teacher of personal finances. It is his passion to help people accomplish far more than they ever thought possible in this area of their lives. He believes when people are financially free, they are able to do exactly what they have been put on Earth to do - regardless of income potential. He is the founder of I Was Broke. Now I’m Not., an organization that provides financial teaching through live events, print and web resources.

Joseph has authored four books: I Was Broke. Now I’m Not., What Everyone Should Know About Money Before They Enter the Real World, Funded and Free, and Oxen: The Key to an Abundant Harvest.
