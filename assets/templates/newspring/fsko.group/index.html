What is Fuse?

FUSE is the weekly gathering of the Student Ministry of NewSpring Church. We’re dedicated to providing a place for students (6th -12th grade) to connect with God, with other students, and with older committed leaders that are dedicated to seeing students grow closer to Jesus.

We want to engage and reach youth culture, so no matter what campus you’re near, NewSpring has an environment each week for students to meet Jesus and grow closer to Him.

What is FSKO?

FSKO is the annual Fuse Summer Kick Off by the Student Ministry of NewSpring Church. Students in 6th-12th grade are invited to come kick off the summer with games, food, prizes and more at one of our NewSpring Campuses.
