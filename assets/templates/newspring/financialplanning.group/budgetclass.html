Who Needs a Budget?

These learning opportunities are extremely beneficial for folks of all ages (from teen to senior adult). Whether you are struggling with money or you are interested in learning ways to manage your money better, these are for you! Managing money wisely will help you honor God with your finances. Everyone is welcome—all ages, from post high school to senior adults—It isn't necessary to be an owner or attender of NewSpring.

If you have a specific question about these opportunities before signing up, please email us. We're here to help!

"The plans of the diligent lead to profit as surely as haste leads to poverty." Proverbs 21:5

What Should I Expect?

The Budget Class is a 90-minute group learning environment where you will learn Bible-based money management principles. In this class you will learn how to create a budget that works, live within your means and save money, as well as ask any budget questions that you might have.

Our coaches are available to help you take your next steps in becoming a good steward of your personal and/or business finances.
